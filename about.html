About Me

I am a passionate Urban & Regional Planner and GIS Specialist with a deep commitment to leveraging spatial intelligence for sustainable development and evidence-based decision-making.

Professional Background

With experience in physical planning, land surveying, and geospatial analysis, I focus on turning complex spatial data into actionable insights that support meaningful decision-making and better cities.

Areas of Expertise

  • Urban and regional planning frameworks
  • Land use optimization and spatial analysis
  • GIS-based decision support systems
  • Cadastral surveying and geodetic control
  • Sustainable development and environmental planning

Philosophy

I believe effective planning and spatial analysis should be grounded in rigorous methodology and local context. My work integrates technical excellence with stakeholder engagement to deliver solutions that are both scientifically sound and practically implementable.
