About Me

Planner, surveyor-in-training, GIS nerd, and future Spatial AI founder.

I work at the intersection of urban & regional planning, land surveying, and geospatial analytics. My work includes estate masterplans, subdivision schemes, riparian analysis, and spatial policy support in Kenya.

I enjoy turning messy datasets, surveys, and maps into clear decisions for landowners, Saccos, counties, and institutions. Long-term, I’m building Geonex as a Spatial AI engine for African land and infrastructure decisions.
