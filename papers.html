Riparian Zones at a Crossroads: Understanding Land Management for Riparian Reserves

Samuel Njuguna; Dr. Silas M. Muketha, PhD

ISK Pre-AGM Conference • Nairobi • May 2025

Conference presentation examining the legal, ecological, and planning dilemmas surrounding riparian reserves in Kenya. The paper critiques fragmented institutional mandates, inconsistent riparian definitions, and weak spatial delineation, and proposes an integrated framework for riparian governance grounded in planning, land administration, surveying, and GIS.
