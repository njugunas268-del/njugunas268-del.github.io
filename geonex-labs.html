Our Mission

Geonex Labs exists to make spatial intelligence usable in real planning workflows — so decisions on land, infrastructure, and climate resilience are faster, clearer, and defensible.

The Problem We Solve

  • Unclear development decisions: approvals and site selection often lack spatial evidence.
  • Risk-blind growth: floods, riparian encroachment, and slope hazards are addressed too late.
  • Fragmented datasets: planners and developers work with inconsistent basemaps and boundaries.
  • Weak compliance: zoning, ESAs, and buffers are not enforced consistently on the ground.

What We’re Building
