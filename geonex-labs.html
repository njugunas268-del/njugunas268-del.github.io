Our Mission

Geonex Labs is dedicated to advancing the field of spatial intelligence through research, innovation, and practical application of cutting-edge geospatial technologies.

We focus on developing tools and methodologies that make complex spatial analytics accessible to planners, policymakers, and communities.

Research Areas

  • AI-powered land use classification
  • Risk-based spatial decision systems
  • Climate-responsive urban planning frameworks
  • Open-source GIS tools for developing contexts
